World of Tanks

In the latest update to World of Tanks, 1.21.1, a new branch was added to the Polish techtree, the tank destroyers and their unique deep-rifled guns that fire star-shaped rounds that deal more damage the closer the target is! Crews have ahd a rework so now they start off at 100% and some changes to crew efficiencies plus some rebalancing of tier 10 vehicles and some map adjustments!

You go around collecting resources to be able to bring back to your extraction point but be careful as a massive sandstorm is rapidly approaching and when it hits, no one will be making it out alive!
